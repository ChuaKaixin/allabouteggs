How to tell if an egg is fresh

How...

Steps

  • Put the egg in a bowl of water

The egg is

  • FRESH - If it stays at the bottom by the side
  • TURNING BAD - If it tilts onto its tip
  • TURNED BAD - If it floats to the top

What...

Why...

Air goes into the egg as time goes by. When enough air goes into the egg, the egg floats indicating that the egg has been around for too long.
